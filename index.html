Design Explorations

10 different design directions for the Agrova farm operations app. Each concept includes full desktop owner dashboard and mobile worker PWA screens with Turkish mock data.

Click any card to open the interactive prototype in a new tab.

Design 01
Earth & Harvest
Warm earthy tones with terracotta, clay, and rich soil colors. Tuscan countryside meets modern SaaS. Handcrafted, organic feel.
Warm Earthy Terracotta Organic
Design 02
Nordic Frost
Clean Scandinavian minimalism with cool blue-gray tones and ice-blue accents. Frosted glass sidebar, maximum breathing room.
Cool Minimal Scandinavian Glass
Design 03
Dark Canopy
Dark mode first, inspired by the shade under a dense tree canopy. Deep greens and charcoals with vivid neon-green glow accents.
Dark Neon Glow Forest
Design 04
Sunrise Fields
Warm, optimistic design inspired by golden hour over farmland. Rich gradients from deep orange to golden yellow. Mediterranean morning energy.
Gradient Warm Sunrise Optimistic
Design 05
Clean Slate
Ultra-minimal, almost monochrome with a single green accent. Maximum whitespace, typography-driven. Inspired by Vercel and Apple.
Minimal Monochrome Typography Clean
Design 06
Olive Grove
Mediterranean elegance with sage greens, olive tones, and warm stone. Serif typography for headings gives a premium, editorial feel.
Mediterranean Serif Elegant Premium
Design 07
Blueprint
Technical, engineering-inspired with grid patterns and precise lines. Monospace numbers, blueprint grid background. Precision and structure.
Technical Grid Monospace Precise
Design 08
Lavender Dusk
Soft, calming twilight design with purple and violet tones and warm pink accents. Approachable and modern, like Arc browser meets Notion.
Purple Soft Calming Modern
Design 09
Anadolu
Turkish-inspired celebrating Anatolian heritage. Deep red, Iznik turquoise, golden amber with subtle geometric kilim border patterns.
Turkish Cultural Geometric Bold
Design 10
Glass Garden
Modern glassmorphism with layered translucent panels over a green gradient. Like looking through a greenhouse. Airy, layered, futuristic.
Glass Translucent Layered Futuristic
